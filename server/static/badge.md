# Badge

<img src="/static/favicon.svg" height="144px" width="144px" />

Notability is summarised in a circular "badge".
The outer ring indicates overall notability
The inside of the ring is divided into four regions with an "H" shape.
The left section indicates N1 (sitelinks).
The central section indicates the two parts of N2, with the top being identifiers and the bottom being sources.
The right section indicates N3 (structural need)

Sections are coloured red for **none**, orange for **weak**, and green for **strong** notability. 
In some circumstances, a section may be grey for **unknown**, indicating that the information is not available or there was some error.
If the item has no claims whatsoever, then both N2 sections will be coloured white for **empty**.

If the QID item is a **redirect**, then a purple circular arrow (not shown) will be superimposed.
In this case, N3 will be evaluated in the normal way,
but N1 and N2 will be evaluated based on the target of the redirection.

If the item is **deleted**, then additional red markers (not shown) appear in the corners.  The item may still retain N3 notability.
